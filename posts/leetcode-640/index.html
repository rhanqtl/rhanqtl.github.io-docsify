LeetCode 640. 求解方程

August 20, 2022 阅读时间：1 分钟

方程的文法如下：

equation   ::= side "=" side
side       ::= operand 
             | operand operator side
operand    ::= number | number "x" | "x"
number     ::= one_nine digits
one_nine   ::= 1 | 2 | ... | 9
digits     ::= 0 | one_nine
operator   ::= "+" | "-"
